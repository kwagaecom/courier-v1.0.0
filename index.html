SERVICES

Our services for you

Standard Courier Services
  • Provides basic delivery services within a country.
  • Typically takes 2–5 business days.
  • Suitable for non-urgent deliveries.
Same-Day Courier Services
  • Delivers packages on the same day they are sent.
  • Often used for urgent or time-sensitive deliveries.
  • Usually limited to shorter distances or within the same city.
International Courier Services
  • Provides delivery across countries or continents.
  • Includes options like express, economy, and freight services.
  • Handles customs and border clearance.
Express Courier Services
  • Faster delivery, often within 1–2 days.
  • Typically more expensive but ensures speed for urgent deliveries.
  • Usually limited to shorter distances or within the same city.
Freight Courier Services
  • For large, bulky, or heavy shipments.
  • Can handle truckload (TL), less-than-truckload (LTL), or air freight.
  • Suitable for businesses shipping large quantities or oversized items.
Specialized Courier Services
  • Tailored for specific industries (e.g., medical, legal).
  • Handles sensitive, confidential, or high-value shipments.
  • May include temperature-controlled, secure, or hazardous materials transport.
Pallet Courier Services
  • For transporting goods on pallets.
  • Often used for bulk shipments.
  • Ensures stability and protection during transport.
On-Demand Courier Services
  • Provides deliveries at any time when needed.
  • Usually for urgent, one-off shipments that need immediate attention.
Overnight Courier Services
  • Offers next-day delivery
  • Ideal for time-sensitive shipments that don't require same-day delivery
